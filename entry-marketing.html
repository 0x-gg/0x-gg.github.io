Entry

Build nouns. Trace the board. Take the lead.

Entry is a focused word strategy game where every letter changes the position. Start from a five-letter noun, add one letter beside the board, and build a connected English noun for points.

One board, many ways to play

  • Play the bot in a quick solo match.
  • Pass and play on one iPhone.
  • Challenge friends with Game Center turn-based multiplayer.
  • Earn achievements, climb leaderboards, and replay your best moves.
