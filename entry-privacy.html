Entry Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 7, 2026

Entry is designed to be a private word strategy game. The app does not include ads, third-party analytics, tracking SDKs, or a separate account system.

Data Collection

Entry does not collect personal data from solo play. Solo games and local game state stay on your device.

Game Center

If you choose multiplayer, Entry uses Apple Game Center for authentication, matchmaking, and turn-based match data. Game Center is provided by Apple and is governed by Apple's terms and privacy policy.

Purchases and Advertising

Entry does not contain in-app purchases or advertising.
