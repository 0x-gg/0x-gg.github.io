Entry Support

Word strategy on a compact 5x5 board.

How to Play

Start from the five-letter center word. Add one letter next to existing letters, then trace a connected English noun that includes the new letter. Each letter in the submitted word scores one point.

Controls

  • Tap a highlighted board cell, then tap a keyboard letter.
  • Tap a keyboard letter, then tap a highlighted board cell.
  • Drag a keyboard letter into a highlighted board cell.
  • Tap board letters to choose the reading path.
  • Use Clear to reset the current word attempt.
  • Use Pass to skip your turn. If the same player passes three times in a row, that player loses.

Multiplayer

Multiplayer uses Apple Game Center turn-based matches. Sign into Game Center on your device, open Multiplayer, and invite a friend.
